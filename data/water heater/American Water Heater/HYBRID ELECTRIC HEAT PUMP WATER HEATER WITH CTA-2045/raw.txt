HYBRID ELECTRIC HEAT PUMP WATER
HEATER WITH CTA-2045

The hybrid electric heat pump water heater from American is the most
cost effective energy-efficient option available for consumers who want
to save money on their utility bills. Hybrid can reduce water heating costs
up to 73% generating annual savings of $306 or more. These models are
smart grid ready for CTA-2045-A utility communication.

HOW DO THEY WORK?

· Absorb ambient heat from the surrounding air to heat water using a compressor
   and environmentally-friendly R134a refrigerant

   · Self-contained heat pump unit is integrated into the top of the tank

   · Multiple operating modes to maximize efficiency and performance
